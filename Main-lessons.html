Lessons

Piano Foundation
 For beginners with 0-2 years of piano learning.
Finger and body postures, exposure to music and rhythm, and basic music theory are taught.

ABRSM Graded Exams
 For novice to intermediate learners with minimum 1.5 years of piano learning.
Grade 1 to 5 practical and theory lessons are offered.

Leisure Piano
 For any interested individuals who has the determination and passion to learn the piano.
Lessons are tailored according to individual preferences and goals.
Students wishing to pursue leisure piano lessons on a short-term basis are also warmly welcomed.
